  • MWC Shanghai 2015 -- Mobile OS Company Acadine Technologies Receives $100M First-Round Funding from Tsinghua Unigroup International

    15/07/2015

    Shanghai, China – Mobile World Congress – July 15, 2015 – Acadine™ Technologies, a Hong Kong-based startup delivering innovative mobile operating systems for mobile, wearable, and IoT devices, announced today that it has received $100M US dollars in its first round financing from Tsinghua Unigroup International, a Hong Kong-based investment company with extensive holdings in the technology sector.

    “We are extremely pleased with this vote of confidence in our team, as it enables us to move forward at the super fast pace demanded by the exploding innovations in the mobile, wearable, and IoT space,” said Dr. Li Gong, Founder, Chairman, and CEO of Acadine Technologies. “With significant operations starting up in Beijing, Taipei, Palo Alto, and London, and with a rapidly growing international team of passionate and experienced mobile experts, we are well positioned to meet the diverse technical, product, and business needs from OEM device makers, telecom operators, as well as application, service, and content providers, and of course consumers.”

    Acadine’s core product is an innovative mobile operating system called H5OS™, which is based on HTML5, the open standard technologies of the modern Internet. A completely web-centric operating system, H5OS eliminates the native APIs that give the conventional mobile OS owners full business control over other mobile industry participants. As a result, H5OS offers the huge potential of a simpler technical architecture, more efficient memory and power consumption, and completely dynamic and seamless interactions between the mobile operating system, on-device applications, and the millions of web apps and services on the Internet at large. The H5OS open architecture also brings about an open mobile ecosystem with open participation, thus enabling choices for everyone, from device makers to operators to consumers, to freely select and experience the best of the web.

    “I am very excited to support Dr. Gong and the team he has assembled to establish a truly open mobile operating system,” said Mr. Zhao Weiguo, Chairman of the Tsinghua Unigroup, parent company of Tsinghua Unigroup International. “The operating system is the most critical link between users and service providers, and this field is the most important battleground for the entire IT industry worldwide. Yet it has been highly monopolized on the desktop and in the mobile space, and the offerings cannot meet the specialized needs of many vertical sectors. The Acadine team has the international vision and caliber to challenge this monopoly and we fully support them in this endeavor.”

    About Acadine.
    Acadine™ Technologies is a startup company specializing in innovative mobile operating systems for mobile, wearable, and IoT devices. Acadine is based in Hong Kong, with operations in Beijing, Taipei, Palo Alto, and London. For more information, please contact press@acadine.com.
