• WHO WE ARE

    ACADINE is an independent provider of operating systems and software / services platforms for new generations of smart devices worldwide.

  • WHY WE ARE HERE

    Today, participants in the IoT revolution are faced with choosing a solution among the highly fragmented incompatible standards or being locked into limited and biased platforms maintained by dominant companies. ACADINE offers a better alternative. Our platform gives control back to the device/platform by enabling customization and innovation amongst developers, content providers, and manufacturers. We believe in open standards to prevent market monopolies and allow smart devices to reach their full potential through seamless communication and connectivity across different hardware platforms.
